ABOUT US

A team of 5 individual which came up with an idea to develop a web application which would help people in challenging times to stay calm and analyse their symptoms at home. mediGo is a platform which focuses on symptom analysis and provides near by help to people in the form of pharmacies in the time of need. Our motto is to tranform every citizen of India to be "Aatmanirbhar" towards their health.

Symptoms

A symptom is a departure from normal function or feeling which is apparent to a patient, reflecting the presence of an unusual state, or of a disease. A symptom can be subjective or objective. Tiredness is a subjective symptom whereas cough or fever are objective symptoms.In contrast to a symptom, a sign is a clue to a disease elicited by an examiner or a doctor
